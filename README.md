# fries-and-chicken
A game where you are a cook and serve customers, but some customers are acting weird...
WARNING: Game contains horror elements.
# Tutorial
Since you are probably new to this game, I'll give you a tutorial.
To figure out how to cook/bake the food the customer wants, go to the Recipe Book and find the page that has the food they want on it. Follow the instructions carefully. Money is an essential resource. Once you have 1M, you can finally quit your job. Upgrades help you get Money faster.
## What is Money?
Money is the resource used for buying upgrades,paying specific amounts of money everyday, and buying ingredients from the store. If you don't pay every day, you have to close your restaurant. Upgrades include:
- More ingredients
- More expensive foods to get more money
- Automated robots to cook for you
and much more.
## Ingredients
Ingredients are brought from the store. You must have ingredients to make the food the customer wants.
## Food
Food is made with ingredients. If the food is amazing, the customer will give you 5 stars. If the food is good, then 3 stars. If the food is bad, 1 star.
### Ratings
Ratings are entirely dependent on the food. Ratings improve the amount of customers to come, so manage it correctly. If your rating is 1 star, you will get below 3 customers per day. Lessening your amount of Money. If your rating is 2, you will get below 10 customers a day. If its 3, you will get above 15 customers per day but below 20. If its 4, you will get above 25 customers but below 30. If its 5, you will get above 60 customers per day but below 90 customers.
## Upgrades
Upgrades are for improving efficiency, rating, speed, and more. If an upgrade can be bought above 1 time, it gets more expensive until you max it out.
## Customers
Some Customers are normal. Depending on how you make the food, they will rate it. In some cases, special customers can come.
### Fake Customers
Some customers are not real. You must hold E to reject them. If you let a fake customer get their food and go into the Dining Room to eat, they will attack normal customers. To stop them, you have to repeatedly press P to punch the fake customer. In some scenarios, the fake customer will attack you and decrease your health. If your health hits 0, you die.
### Special Customers
Special customers are ones that can:
- Boost money by multipliers
- Form a group of people to eat your food
- Explain how customers would like a food
# The Ending
Quitting your job at the end is essentially hitting the Rebirth button in a game. Your rating, upgrades, money, multipliers, and ingredients will be reset.
